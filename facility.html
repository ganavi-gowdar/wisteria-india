Campfire

Our campfire experience is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy the great outdoors. Imagine sitting by the fire, feeling the warmth, and listening to the soothing sounds of the river stream nearby. It's an amazing way to unwind and connect with nature.

Parking

We offer our guests private parking within our premises, ensuring their vehicles are safe and secure. Our priority is to provide convenience and peace of mind during your visit.

Trekking

We offer an exciting trekking adventure at Nishani Motte with the added convenience of Jeep pick up and drop off. Get ready to experience the breathtaking beauty of the mountains and immerse yourself in nature. Don't miss out on this exhilarating opportunity!
